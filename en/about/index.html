Derived Cat
Interested in Math, Artificial Intelligence and Computer Graphics

This is Derived Cat ~ Welcome to my website ~

Since 2016, most of my notes have been typeset by $\LaTeX$ and shared on Github, mainly including mathematics, machine learning and economics. On the one hand, I wanted to learning WebGL. On the other hand, I’d like to keep some less formal notes capturing my immature ideas. Accordingly, I began to use personal blog ~

The content of this website mainly involves mathematics and applied mathematics, artificial intelligence and machine learning, computer graphics. The articles here don’t really pursue abstruse things, but hope to have some ingenuity indeed. There are countless pearls hidden in the sea of Internet, while the density of the information that really help also remains extremely low. My only wish is to more or less write something original and to provide some new perspective, hopefully serving as an assistance rather than interference for others ~

In addition to these articles, I also provide some WebGL demos on the Demo page. You can right-click to view the source code directly, or view the corresponding codes on Github. As a lightweight graphic API, WebGL is very suitable for proof of concept and reproducing papers. And of course, it can smooth the steep learning curve of modern graphics API ~

Thanks to the Hexo framework and Fluid theme developed by predecessors, people who are not familiar with Web technology like me can quickly publish their own websites ~
